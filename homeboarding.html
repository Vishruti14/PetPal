What is Dog Home Boarding or Doggy Boarding?


So much more fun than kennels!

We offer a home doggy boarding service where your dog can stay in the home of one of our carefully selected and dedicated host families. Our host families provide the magic ingredient needed to ensure your dog is settled in, cared for, receiving all the cuddles and home comforts they are used to, a real ‘home from home’ experience. Both Petpals and their host families will keep you updated with how things are going with regular photographs of their experience providing you with peace of mind so you can relax and enjoy your time away.
