PetPals About US

Petpals are proud to be the No.1 award-winning multi-service pet care company in the UK. We were founded in 2001 and since then have grown to cover over 150 areas nationwide. We pride ourselves on delivering the highest standards of ethical pet care in the UK. Every single person working at Petpals is a true pet lover, this is what makes us so good at what we do. We understand that sometimes work and life get in the way and people need a helping hand looking after their pets, which is where we come in. From dogs, cats and rabbits to budgies, gerbils and chickens, we cover it all. We offer a full range of pet care services, including pet sitting, small pet boarding, dog walking, dog home boarding, puppy care and elderly dog care. We operate in specially developed pet friendly and safe vehicles. Whether you require regular or occasional pet care, we can tailor our services to meet you and your pets specific needs. For your peace of mind all of our Petpals owners are DBS checked, insured, first aid trained and uniformed. We are trusted by thousands of people across the UK every day to look after their pets and we hope to welcome you and your pets into the Petpals family very soon.
